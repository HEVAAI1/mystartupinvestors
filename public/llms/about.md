# About MyFundingList — How It Works

**MyFundingList** is a startup investor discovery and outreach platform. We give founders direct, verified access to angels, VCs, syndicates, and funds — so they can raise funding faster and smarter.

→ [Start for Free](https://www.myfundinglist.com)

---

## Our Mission

Fundraising is broken for most founders. The process is opaque, slow, and heavily dependent on warm introductions that most founders don't have access to.

MyFundingList democratizes investor access. Whether you're a first-time founder in Bangalore or a serial entrepreneur in New York, you get the same verified investor data that was previously only available through expensive advisors or closed networks.

---

## Who We're Built For

- **Pre-seed & seed stage startups** looking for their first institutional check
- **Bootstrapped founders** ready to bring on investors
- **Second-time founders** running efficient, targeted fundraising processes
- **Startup studios & incubators** helping portfolio companies connect with investors
- **Investment bankers & advisors** sourcing deals and connections for clients

---

## The MyFundingList Approach

### 1. Verified Data, Not Scraped Noise
Every investor in our database has been manually verified or validated through reliable sources. We update the database **weekly** to keep emails accurate and profiles current.

### 2. Direct Access, No Gatekeepers
No waiting for intros. No LinkedIn connection requests. You get the investor's direct email address and you reach out on your own terms.

### 3. Smart Filtering
Filter investors by:
- **Industry / Sector** (SaaS, Fintech, HealthTech, EdTech, D2C, DeepTech, etc.)
- **Investment Stage** (Pre-seed, Seed, Series A, etc.)
- **Geography** (India, USA, Southeast Asia, Europe, MENA, etc.)
- **Investor Type** (Angel, VC Fund, Syndicate, Family Office, Corporate VC)

### 4. Founder-First Tools
Credits work across both the investor database and our Tools for Founders suite — giving you a complete fundraising toolkit in one platform.

---

## How Credits Work

Each credit unlocks **one verified investor contact** — revealing their direct email address and full profile details.

- Credits **never expire**
- Credits are shared across investor unlocks and startup tools
- No subscriptions, no auto-renewals (except where chosen)
