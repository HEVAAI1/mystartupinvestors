# Investor Discovery

MyFundingList provides access to a curated database of investors.

## Features

- Verified investor profiles
- Filters:
  - Stage (Pre-seed, Seed, Series A)
  - Sector (SaaS, Fintech, AI, etc.)
  - Geography
- Contact and outreach support

## Benefits

- Saves hours of manual research
- Improves investor-founder fit
- Enables targeted outreach

## Best Practices

- Match investor thesis with your startup
- Personalize outreach messages
- Focus on relevance over volume
