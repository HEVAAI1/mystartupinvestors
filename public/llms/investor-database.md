## Investor Types Included

### Venture Capital Firms
Full VC funds ranging from pre-seed micro-funds to multi-stage global funds. Includes partners, associates, and principals with verified direct emails.

### Angel Investors
Individual angels with verified contact information, investment thesis, check sizes, and sector preferences.

### Syndicates
Angel syndicates and rolling funds that co-invest with a lead — ideal for startups looking for community-backed rounds.

### Family Offices
Private family offices actively deploying capital into startups and growth-stage companies.

### Corporate Venture Capital (CVC)
Strategic investors from large corporations looking for startup partnerships, pilots, and investments.
