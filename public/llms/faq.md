## About the Platform

**What is MyFundingList?**
MyFundingList is a startup investor discovery platform. It provides founders with access to a verified database of 5,000+ investors — including angels, VCs, syndicates, and funds — along with their direct email addresses and profile information.

**Who is MyFundingList for?**
It's built for startup founders who are actively fundraising or preparing to raise. It's particularly useful for pre-seed and seed stage startups, but also works well for Series A and growth-stage companies.

**Is MyFundingList only for Indian startups?**
No. While we have a strong community of Indian founders, the investor database covers 120+ global cities. Founders from any country can use the platform to connect with investors globally.

**How is MyFundingList different from Crunchbase, AngelList, or LinkedIn?**
MyFundingList provides **verified direct email addresses** for investors — not just profiles. You can reach investors without needing a premium subscription, a warm intro, or InMail credits. Our data is also specifically curated and updated for founder outreach.

## Investor Database

**How many investors are in the database?**
Over 5,000 verified investors as of the latest update. The number grows regularly.

**What types of investors are included?**
Angels, VC funds, micro-VCs, syndicates, family offices, and corporate venture capital (CVC) arms.

**How often is the investor data updated?**
The database is updated **weekly** to keep contact information accurate and profiles current.

**Can I filter investors by industry, stage, or location?**
Yes. Advanced filters are available on Professional, Growth, and Enterprise plans. You can filter by sector, investment stage, geography, investor type, and more.

**What if an investor email bounces?**
If a verified email bounces, contact our support team. We'll investigate and replace the credit if the data is found to be inaccurate.

## Credits & Billing

**How do credits work?**
Each credit unlocks one verified investor contact — revealing their direct email address and full profile details. Credits also apply to Tools for Founders usage.

**Do credits expire?**
No. Credits on all paid plans never expire. You can use them at your own pace.

**Can I buy more credits after I've used mine?**
Yes. You can purchase additional credit packs at any time from your account dashboard.

**Is the Starter plan really free?**
Yes. The Starter plan is permanently free and gives you 5 investor contact unlocks per month — no credit card required.

**Can I get a refund on unused credits?**
Unused credits are non-refundable, but they remain valid indefinitely. They will not disappear.

**What payment methods are accepted?**
All major credit and debit cards. Payments are processed securely.

## Outreach & Fundraising

**Does MyFundingList send emails on my behalf?**
No. MyFundingList gives you the investor's verified contact information. You compose and send your own emails. This keeps your outreach personal and authentic.

**Can I export investor contacts to my CRM or email tool?**
Yes. CSV export is available on Professional, Growth, and Enterprise plans.

**Can I save investor lists for future reference?**
Yes. Save lists are available on Growth and Enterprise plans.

**What is the Investment Banking add-on?**
The $499 IB add-on is available on Growth and Enterprise plans. It gives you dedicated human support from our team — including investor matching, pitch deck review, outreach strategy, and warm introductions where possible.

## Adding Your Startup

**How do I add my startup to MyFundingList?**
There is a startup submission form on the platform. Fill in your details and our team will review your application. Exceptional startups may also receive manual fundraising assistance.

**Does adding my startup guarantee investment?**
No. Adding your startup increases visibility within the platform's network, but funding decisions are made by investors independently.
