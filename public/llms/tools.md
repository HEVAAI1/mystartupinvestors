# Tools for Founders — Startup Toolkit by MyFundingList

MyFundingList is more than an investor database. We offer a suite of **startup tools** designed to help founders at every stage of their fundraising journey — from validating their pitch to managing outreach.

→ [Explore Tools](https://www.myfundinglist.com/tools-for-founders)

---

## What Are Startup Tools?

Startup tools are built-in utilities available to all MyFundingList users. Tool usage is powered by the same credit system as investor contact unlocks — giving you a unified, flexible toolkit.

Credits work across:
- Investor contact unlocks
- Startup tool usage

---

## Tools Available

### Pitch Deck Analyzer
Upload or describe your pitch deck and get structured feedback on narrative flow, missing slides, and investor readiness. Know exactly what to fix before you hit send.

### Investor Match Finder
Input your startup's stage, sector, and geography and get a curated list of investors most likely to be interested — powered by our database filters and matching logic.

### Cold Email Generator
Generate personalized, high-converting cold email templates for investor outreach. Tailored to your startup, your round, and the investor's focus area.

### Fundraising Checklist
A comprehensive, stage-by-stage checklist covering everything from pre-seed prep to term sheet negotiation. Know exactly where you are and what comes next.

### Startup Valuation Estimator
Get a ballpark valuation range for your startup based on comparable deals, traction metrics, and stage — useful for anchoring conversations with investors.

### Cap Table Planner
Model how dilution will work across your seed and Series A rounds. Understand your ownership before you sign anything.
